papier machine

website

Papier Machine was a kickstarter project launched in 2016 by three French designers. Papier Machine made its first public appearance in 2017, at the Musées des Arts décoratifs in Paris. This was our first chance to present and explain our product to the world. We wanted it to be an interactive experience and allow visitor to try, test and play with Papier Machine. After collecting feedback from our supporters and industry leaders, we got back to work and develop the final product.
